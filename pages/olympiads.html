Olympiad preparation

Train like an economist, write like a finalist.

Olympiad success comes from model fluency, careful diagrams, clean assumptions, and fast reflection after mistakes.

12-week roadmap

A realistic plan for students balancing school, contests, and deep study.

Weeks 1-2: Foundations

Scarcity, opportunity cost, demand, supply, GDP, inflation, and graph hygiene. Solve short drills daily.

Weeks 3-5: Core models

Elasticity, market structures, fiscal policy, monetary policy, exchange rates, and AD-AS. Write one paragraph explanation after every graph.

Weeks 6-8: Advanced reasoning

Game theory, externalities, IS-LM, growth, inequality, and policy evaluation. Practice mixed sets under time pressure.

Weeks 9-12: Contest simulation

Full mock papers, post-mortems, formula recall, and essay outlines. Track recurring errors and rebuild weak models.
